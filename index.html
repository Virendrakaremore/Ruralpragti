The Rural Education and Training Platform is an online resource hub dedicated to empowering rural communities.

It offers educational materials and training programs focused on modern farming techniques, financial management, and digital literacy.

The platform aims to support sustainable development by providing practical knowledge and skills that improve livelihoods and help rural individuals and communities adapt to evolving challenges in agriculture and technology.
